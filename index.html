Fa Mulan, from the disney film "Mulan", is the 8th official Disney Princess. In order to replace her father in the Imperial Army, Mulan pretendss as a male soldier. She finds it difficult to blend in with her soldiers, but she forms an improbable friendship with her brave leader, Li Shang.

  • Courageous
  • Passionate
  • Determined
  • Assertive


Rapunzel, from the disney film "Tangled", is the 10th official Disney Princess. For years, a teenager with seventy feet of golden hair has been confined to a tower. Once she meets one of the most wanted and equally charming bandits in her kingdom, she embarks on a hair-raising escape.

  • Intelligent
  • Charming
  • Energetic/Outgoing
  • Optimistic


Elsa the Snow Queen of Arendelle, from the disney film "Frozen", not counted as a princess, for she is a queen. When Elsa discovers she has frosty superpowers, she flees. Her sister, Anna, who is always hopeful, sets off on a quest to locate her sister and assist in rescuing their realm from perpetual winter.

  • Caring
  • Fearful
  • Powerful
  • Mysterious


Agrabah Princess Jasmine, from the disney film "Aladdin", is the 6th official Disney Princess. In order to defend their realm from the malevolent sorcerer Jafar, she meets the charming and clever Aladdin and partners up with her, a long side with a humorous Genie.

  • Compassionate
  • Caring
  • Independent
  • Assertive


Moana Waialiki of Motunui, from the disney film "Moana", is the 12th official Disney Princess. Unaware that she is going on a personal voyage of self-discovery, Moana, an adventurous girl, sets out on a quest to fulfill an ancient quest of her ancestors.

  • Adventurous
  • Caring
  • Determined
  • Strong
